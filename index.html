Contributing to Open Source - 22 Jul 2022

As an NDA's SUMO, Social, and KB contributor at Mozilla, I have learned heaps! And I mean it. I believe open source software is certainly the best and the open source community is awesome!

This week, I have decided to also contribute to OpenLibrary and Discourse as I use Internet Archive a lot watching old Hollywood films and meta Discourse is such a nice community and the company's ethics are just great.

I was a librarian in fact at my local primary school library (past student) which had around 15,000 books so contributing to OpenLibrary is rather meaningful to me. Also literature and history are one of my favourite subjects.

And most importantly, you learn while contributing and it also gives you experience. I can certainly say I am learning everyday by contributing to various open source projects.
